# Twilight: The Game 

## Story
My game is set in the town of Forks, Washington. Folk stories tell the history of werewolves and vampires in this area. Do they still roam freely? Let's find out. 

You are Bella Swan. You have just uncovered the secret. There are still werewolves and vampires in Forks. In fact, you have fallen in love with a vampire and a werewolf has fallen in love with you. Your love triangle begins to cause tension between the vampires and werewolves. You must survive the wrath of all the enemies around you while trying to get a ring from your super hot vampire bf so he can finally make you a vampire forever. 

## How to Play

### Running the Game
```bash
python game.py
```

### Commands
help — Shows all available commands.

look — Gives a description of your current location, including enemies, items, and exits.

go [direction] — Moves you in a direction. Example: go north

north, south, east, west — Shortcut movement commands.

fight or attack — Starts combat with an enemy in your current location.

take [item] — Picks up an item in the location. Example: take cullen ring

inventory or i — Shows the items you are carrying.

quit — Exits the game.

## Goal
Reach 100 Vampire Progress

Pick up the Cullen Ring

Once both conditions are met, you achieve victory: Bella becomes a vampire and marries her vampire love interest.

## Tips
Explore every location carefully because important items are hidden around the map.

Pick up useful items when you see them.

Fight enemies to increase your Vampire Progress.

The Cullen Ring is required to win, so make sure you find it before the end.

Some enemies are stronger than others, so be careful when choosing when to fight.

If your health reaches 0, the game is over.

Running away can save your life if you are losing a battle.
